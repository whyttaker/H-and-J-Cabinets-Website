Our Approach

Vanities built for
daily life and lasting beauty

The bathroom demands more from its cabinetry than almost any other room in the home. Constant humidity, direct water contact, and daily use over decades — these are the conditions custom bathroom vanities must be built to withstand. At H & J Cabinets, we engineer for that reality from the first cut.

We design every vanity and bathroom cabinet to your exact space and specification. Sink configuration, storage layout, drawer depth, door profile, finish — all of it is determined by your needs and the character of the room, not by what happens to be available in a catalogue.

Built to Last

Moisture-resistant construction
that holds its integrity

Bathroom cabinetry fails when it is built the same way as kitchen or bedroom cabinetry. We don't make that mistake. Every bathroom cabinet we produce is built with moisture-resistant materials throughout — marine-grade plywood for box construction, solid wood face frames, and finish systems selected specifically for high-humidity environments.

  • Marine-grade plywood construction Engineered to resist swelling, delamination, and warping in sustained humidity — the conditions a bathroom produces every single day.
  • Solid hardwood face frames Kiln-dried and carefully selected to remain stable through seasonal humidity changes.
  • Catalyzed finish systems Conversion varnishes and catalyzed lacquers that seal the wood completely against moisture penetration.
  • Undermount and integrated sink options We build to accommodate stone, quartz, or solid-surface tops — coordinated with your countertop fabricator from the design stage.
