Serving All of Orange County

Orange County's premier
custom cabinetry builder

H & J Cabinets is based in Santa Ana and has served homeowners, interior designers, architects, and custom builders across Orange County for over 30 years. From Newport Beach and Corona del Mar on the coast to Irvine, Mission Viejo, and Anaheim Hills inland, we have built custom cabinetry in some of the county's most distinguished residences.

Orange County is home to one of the most active luxury real estate markets in the United States, and the standards for interior finish quality are among the highest anywhere. Our work is designed for that environment, built with materials and methods that reflect the quality the county's finest homes demand.

Why It Matters Here

A premium market
with premium expectations

Orange County consistently ranks among the wealthiest counties in the nation. Its homeowners invest substantially in their properties and expect every element of a finished home, including cabinetry, to reflect the overall quality of the residence. In this market, the difference between standard cabinetry and truly custom cabinetry is both visible and meaningful.

  • Coastal and inland communities Orange County spans coastal cities with specific humidity and salt air considerations, and inland communities with their own climate and design characteristics. Our materials and construction methods are chosen to perform across all of these environments.
  • A diverse range of home styles From traditional estates in Coto de Caza to contemporary residences in Irvine to beachside homes in Laguna Beach, Orange County homes reflect a wide range of architectural styles. Custom cabinetry is built to match each one precisely.
  • High-value real estate market In a county where median home prices are among the highest in California, interior finish quality has a direct impact on property value. Custom cabinetry built to last is an investment that pays long-term dividends.
Our Approach

Materials, craftsmanship,
and 30 years of experience

We build every Orange County project using traditional face-frame construction, the method that provides structural integrity over the long term. Every cabinet box is built from premium hardwoods and quality plywood, with no MDF in any structural area. The result is cabinetry that holds its quality across decades of use.

Our own team manages every phase of every project. No subcontractors. No handoffs. The same level of care that goes into the design is maintained through fabrication and installation.
