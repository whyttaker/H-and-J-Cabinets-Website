About This Residence

Traditional Precision on the Newport Cliffs

The Newport Cliff Residence demonstrates how traditional cabinetry achieves its distinction through the accumulation of carefully considered details. Situated on the cliffs above the Pacific in Newport Beach, this home set a brief of traditional excellence with no concessions — every detail to be custom-designed, every surface to be hand-finished, every functional element to be integrated invisibly.

The most technically demanding elements of this project are the custom-styled toe kicks, the inlaid panel work, and the flush inset refrigerator surrounds that integrate the kitchen's major appliances into the cabinetry wall with seamless precision. Each of these details requires a higher level of shop production skill and site installation accuracy than standard cabinetry.

H & J's face-frame construction provides the structural integrity that makes flush inset work possible and durable. The geometry of inset cabinet doors and drawers is unforgiving — any variation in box dimension reads immediately in the installed work. H & J's production team achieves the tolerances this method demands through a combination of precision tooling, careful material handling, and decades of accumulated craft knowledge.

For clients in Newport Beach seeking traditional cabinetry at the highest level of craft, the Newport Cliff Residence represents the standard H & J holds itself to on every project. We are particularly well-suited to homes where the complexity of the brief is matched by the ambition of the owner.
