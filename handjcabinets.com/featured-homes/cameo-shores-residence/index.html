About This Residence

Sophisticated Contemporary on the Corona del Mar Shore

Cameo Shores is among the most architecturally distinguished communities in Corona del Mar — a neighborhood where homes are expected to be as refined as the setting. The Cameo Shores Residence gave H & J Cabinets the opportunity to produce contemporary cabinetry at a level that matched the home's architectural ambitions: sleek, precise, and deeply considered.

The design brief for this project centered on creating an interior environment defined by sophisticated restraint. H & J's production team delivered cabinetry with perfectly consistent finish applications, tight construction tolerances, and installation precision that achieves the seamless quality the design intended. The absence of visible fasteners, consistent reveals, and flush panel alignment give this home's cabinetry a quality that reads as architectural rather than merely functional.

Every design decision in this home was tested against the question of whether it would still feel right in twenty years. Material selections favor species and finishes with proven longevity; construction methods prioritize structural integrity over surface effect. H & J's framed construction provides the stability that keeps contemporary cabinetry looking exactly as designed for decades.

The Cameo Shores Residence articulates H & J's position clearly: we are not the right choice for every client, but for those who demand precision, material integrity, and craft that will outlast trends, we are the right choice. For clients in Corona del Mar with similar standards, we welcome the conversation.
